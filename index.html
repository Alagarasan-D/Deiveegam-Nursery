About Our Natural Growing Process

At Deiveegam Nursery, we grow our coconut plants using completely natural and organic methods. We combine traditional techniques with modern knowledge to ensure healthy, high-quality saplings.

Our soil is enriched with organic compost made from cow dung, neem cake, and other natural inputs. We avoid chemical fertilizers and use natural pest control to protect our plants and the environment.

Each sapling is given individual care, from seed selection to transplantation. With drip irrigation and careful water management, our plants develop strong roots and adapt well after planting. The result? Stronger, healthier coconut plants with higher survival and early fruiting — naturally grown, the Deiveegam way.

The result of our natural growing process is evident in the quality of our plants. They have stronger immunity, better adaptability to local conditions, and higher survival rates after transplantation. Our customers consistently report excellent growth and early fruiting from our plants. This is the power of natural, patient, and careful cultivation - the Deiveegam way.
